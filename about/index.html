Images

The Project Renard logo is a picture of the medieval character Reynard which is taken from Book of Hours from circa 1460. The cropped image is taken from this Wikimedia page and the original full page is at this Wikimedia page.

The header image is taken from the border of an illuminated manuscript at the British Library.
